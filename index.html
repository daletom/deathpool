Welcome to the Game of Thrones (GoT) Deathpool!

Rules: You need to make a selection for each character below choosing whether you think they will live or die. For ever correct selection, you will be awarded one point. Once a character dies, this point will be reflected in your score. At the end of the season if a character is still alive, this will be reflected in your score. At the bottom there are three additional bonus questions. You do not lose point for getting this wrong, but if you get it correct you will receive many more points. Cost: This is a $10 cost to enter the GoT Deathpool. Third place will receive 10% of the winnings, Second place will receive 30% of the winnings, and First place will win 60% of the winnings! We will use the email you provide to contact regarding the $10 cost. If you do not pay after three episodes you will be removed from the pool.
